About Me

I am an aspiring Marketing Strategist who as a member of Rotary International, has come to excel in Strategy, Public Relations and Event Coordination. Using platforms in my sphere of contact, I have led results-driven changes in various small teams and leadership roles.

Having my first leadership role as a Youth leader at my local church at the age of 16 and taking on more roles overtime: Choir leader, Rotaract Vice President, Rotaract President and District Rotaract Representative, I have proven to have strong leadership, communication and interpersonal skills.

Novelty, producing excellent results, and making a positive impact are core values with which my work aligns. As a novelty seeker, I set ambitious goals that challenge the status quo; “oh! It’s never been done before? GOOD! Let’s do it!” I work tirelessly to achieve set goals while being cognizant of the processes I use to achieve them; I make every effort to ensure that I yield the best results possible. It’s also very important to me that my work has a positive impact on all stakeholders.

When I am not working on the next big project, I am usually at singing rehearsals, having coffee with friends or reading comics on the internet.
